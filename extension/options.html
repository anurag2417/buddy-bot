Incognito Mode

How to Enable Incognito Monitoring

  1. Open chrome://extensions in your browser
  2. Find "BuddyBot Safety Monitor"
  3. Click "Details"
  4. Toggle "Allow in Incognito" to ON

Without this, the extension cannot monitor Incognito browsing. Children may bypass safety filters using Incognito mode.
